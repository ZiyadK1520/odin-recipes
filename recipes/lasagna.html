Luigis Lasagna

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • 2lbs ground beef
  • 1 white onion
  • 2 cloves of Garlic
  • 1 can crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 block of mozarella cheese

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook 2 pounds of ground beef over medium-high heat until it's browned and no longer pink. Break it into small pieces as it cooks.
  2. Dice the white onion and mince the garlic cloves. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the browned beef and sauté for a few minutes until the onions become translucent. Pour in the can of crushed tomatoes and add 2 tablespoons of sugar to the mixture. Stir well.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally. This allows the flavors to meld together and the sauce to thicken slightly.
  4. Preheat your oven to the temperature indicated on the lasagna noodle package (usually around 350°F or 175°C). In a greased baking dish, start layering the lasagna. Begin with a layer of the meat sauce, followed by a layer of lasagna noodles, a layer of sliced mozzarella cheese, and repeat until you've used up all your ingredients, finishing with a layer of sauce on top.
  5. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until the lasagna is hot and bubbly. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the top is golden and the cheese is melted and slightly browned. Let the lasagna rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
