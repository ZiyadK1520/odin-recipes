Edwards Egg Salad

This egg salad tastes wonderful in a sandwich and will definitely be devoured! It's really good on rye.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2tbsp Mayonaise
  • 1tbsp Hot Sauce
  • 1 Green onion
  • 1tbsp Salt
  • 1tbsp Pepper
  • 1tbsp Paprika

Steps

  1. Boil and peel the eggs, then chop them.
  2. Mix mayonnaise, mustard, salt, pepper, and paprika in a bowl to make the dressing.
  3. Finely chop the green onion.
  4. Combine the chopped eggs, green onion, and dressing in a bowl and mix well.
  5. Spread the egg salad mixture onto bread slices to make a sandwich.
